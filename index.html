Center of Excellence

A Government of Gujarat Award

Marwadi University has been proudly recognized as a "Centre of Excellence" by the Government of Gujarat, placing it among the TOP SEVEN INSTITUTIONS in the state. This prestigious status highlights the university's commitment to providing Quality Education, Excellent Research, Innovative Teaching Methodologies, Quality of Extension Services, High Employability and Entrepreneurship are being imparted.

Our Vision

To cultivate an environment that empowers individuals, organizations, and societies through education, innovative ideas, impactful research, and transformative training.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that fosters social transformation, cultivates leadership through innovation and entrepreneurship, advances research culture, promotes universal consciousness, and drives growth through industrial and international partnerships.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

  • Apply knowledge and competence in the design and development of computer-based systems addressing industrial and social needs.
  • Adapt to emerging technological advancements including Cyber Security, AR / VR, and IoT.

Engineering the World We Want

Marwadi University's Faculty of Engineering and Technology, ranked among the top engineering colleges in Gujarat, offers a dynamic hub of interdisciplinary learning. Here, hands-on experiences and a spirit of innovation challenge conventional thinking. Our close-knit community fosters collaboration between passionate students and engaged faculty, who are driven by a shared commitment to making a real-world impact. Whether in the classroom, lab, or design studio, our engineers are empowered to create and innovate, engineering a better world for generations to come.
